Oleksandr "fallen" Kostyljev

One of the greatest and most accomplished Counter-Strike players of all time

Oleksandr "s1mple" Kostyljev holding a trophy of Player of the Decade Award at IEM Rio Major 2022.

Here's a time line of s1mple life and career:

  • 1997 - Born on 2 October in Ukraine
  • 2001 - Introduce to Counter-Strike by his older brother
  • 2012 - Started playing Counter-Strike Global Offensive on its release
  • 2013 - Made a debut as a pro and joined his first professional Counter-Strike team called LAN DODGERS
  • 2014 - Change team and signed by Courage Gaming, but at the same year s1mple was signed by Hellraisers which has a roster filled with his idol players
  • 2015 - Removed from Hellraisers roster and banned because of cheating. After the ban lifted s1mple got signed by Flipsid3 Tactics but leave the team at the same year
  • 2016 - Studied Chinese at universities then moved to Los Angeles and joined Team Liquid, but leave the team because of homesickness and decided to join NaVi
  • 2017 - Got into the ELEAGUE Major but lost at quaterfinals and awarded the #8 placement in the HLTV top 20
  • 2018 - Finished in 3rd at ELEAGUE Major but won other several tournaments, due to his performance he became the #1 spot at the HLTV top 20 players and achieved the highest individual rating out of all of previous players and considered as the best player in CS:GO by fans
  • 2019 - Only finished in 3rd to 4th at Major but still receiving MVP awards because of his performance
  • 2021 - Won his first major with NaVi and made history by winning every single map played throughout the tournament, s1mple also earned tournament MVP and won Best Esports Player at The Game Awards
  • 2022 - Crowned as Player of the Decade Awards at IEM Rio CS:GO Major
  • 2023 - Decided to stepping away from professional Counter-Strike competition temporarily

"I won't give up playing, improving. I won't lose motivation because of bad comments."
